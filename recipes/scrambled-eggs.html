How to make it!

The secret to making scrambled eggs is all about patience...and the proper pan. Here's a quick step-by-step guide to show you how to make scrambled eggs, plus we'll answer your most frequently asked questions.

Steps

  1. Get the ingredients

    2. To make scrambled eggs for two, you'll need 4 to 6 eggs, 4 to 6 tablespoons of milk (figure 1 tablespoon of milk for every egg), and salt and pepper to taste. Chopped fresh parsley and other herbs add flavor and visual appeal, but they're completely optional.

  2. Break some eggs

    3. Crack eggs on a flat surface (helps ensure shells stay out of the mix) into a bowl that's deep enough for some serious whisking.

  3. Add liquid (optional):

    4. You can thin the scrambled egg mix with milk, cream, lemon juice, or even a little water.

  4. Heat the skillet

    5. To make scrambled eggs on the stove, melt a teaspoon of butter or olive oil — or a combination of both —

  5. Add the eggs and stir

    6. When the butter starts to bubble, or a drop of water added to the pan sizzles, slowly pour in the egg mixture. Immediately reduce the heat to medium-low and as soon as the eggs begin to "set"

  6. Add more flavor

    7. add minced herbs, shredded cheese, crumbled bacon, chopped scallions, sautéed mushrooms, chopped tomato, or anything else you like.

  7. The big finish

    8. Remove the pan from the heat and continue to stir until the eggs finish cooking using only the residual heat from the pan. This helps prevent the eggs from overcooking

Scrambled FAQ

  • What's the best pan for scrambled eggs?

    • Cooking scrambled eggs is easiest in a nonstick skillet

  • How much milk do you put in scrambled eggs?

    • . For creamy scrambled eggs, you'll add up to 1 tablespoon of milk for every egg. For fluffy scrambled eggs, you'll add up to 1 tablespoon of water for every egg.

  • How long does it take to make scrambled eggs?

    • Using only 1 to 3 eggs, it might take you 3 to 5 minutes from the time you pour the egg mixture into the pan

  • How do you make scrambled eggs in a microwave?

    • The most common way to make scrambled eggs in a microwave is by placing one or two whisked eggs in a microwave-safe cup or bowl
